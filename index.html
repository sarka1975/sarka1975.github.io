Elixora is more than just perfume; it’s an experience. Step into our world and create a scent that’s completely your own. From choosing the notes you love to crafting something personal and unique, every step is designed to bring out your true essence.

Discover our characters

Meet the Star, the Midnight, and the Moon—each representing a unique facet of individuality.
These celestial guides inspire our creations and connect deeply with your personal journey. Find the character that resonates with your essence and let them lead you to your perfect scent.

The Star

The Star represents dreams, aspirations, and the magic within. She is independent, optimistic, and passionate, embodying confidence and loyalty. As a guiding light, she inspires others to embrace their true essence and let it shine.

The Midnight

The Midnight is insightful, creative, and thoughtful. She is determined and honest, offering quiet strength and guidance. She inspires those around her to reflect, grow, and embrace their creativity while staying true to their true self.

The Moon

The Moon is intuitive, faithful, and deeply genuine. He embodies calmness, reflecting the quiet strength of night. As a guiding presence, he inspires introspection and emotional connection, encouraging others to find peace and clarity in their own journey.
